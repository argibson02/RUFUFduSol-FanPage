RÜFÜS DU SOL Fan Page

RÜFÜS DU SOL is an alternative dance/electronica band that formed in 2010 in Sydney, Australia. They released their first album Atlas in 2014, and started to gain worldwide recognition with their 2016 album Bloom. Their newest record Solace was released in 2018 and was nominated for a Grammy.

Band Members and Instruments

  • Tyrone Lindqvist – Vocals, Guitar
  • Jon George – Keyboards
  • James Hunt – Drums
